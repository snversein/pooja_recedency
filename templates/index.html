About The Project

Pooja Residency

Pooja Residency is a premium residential plotted development strategically located in Chilhati (P.H. No. 29), Bilaspur.

Developed by Matashri Realities (Partners: Mr. Anil Kumar Agarwal & others), the project spans a total area of 1.5880 Hectares (15,880 sq. mtrs.) under Khasra No. 15003.

Ensuring complete transparency and secure investment, the project is officially approved by Chhattisgarh RERA (Registration No: PCGRERA060426002078) and is valid until March 2031.
